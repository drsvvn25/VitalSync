Frequently Asked Questions

Is my medical data secure?
Yes, VitalSync uses end-to-end encryption and follows strict healthcare data protection standards. Your data is only accessible by you and the doctors you explicitly grant access to.
How do doctors see my vitals?
Once you've registered and selected a doctor, your synchronized health data appears directly on their professional dashboard in real-time.
Can I use VitalSync without health devices?
Absolutely! While we support automated device syncing, you can manually input your vitals (blood pressure, temperature, etc.) at any time.
